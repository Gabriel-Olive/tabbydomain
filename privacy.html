Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

Extension name

Tab Volume by Domain

What data we collect

Only a numeric value (decimal between 0.0 and 1.0) per domain is stored to remember your preferred volume. No personal data, credentials or page content are collected.

How we use this data

The stored value is used to set the volume of media elements (<audio> and <video>) on the corresponding domain when you visit the site. Data is kept locally via the chrome.storage API; it may be synced across your devices if you enable Chrome Sync.
